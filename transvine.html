Specializing in Wine and Winery Equipment Transport - Worldwide.

The transportation of wine, and the technology/equipment used to produce it, continues to evolve to where wine making and distribution is now a truly global enterprise involving a trade infrastructure the early wine makers could never have imagined.

TransVine, the logistics and freight forwarding service of TransGroup, is tailored specifically to the wine industry, specializing in wine and winery equipment transportation throughout North America and the wine regions of the world. Our team of wine transport specialists utilizes an extensive global network of specialized carriers and service providers to handle, store and deliver your product. From harvesters and crushers to barrels and bottles, corks and caps to frozen enzymes and temperature sensitive finished product, TransVine delivers the best wine industry supply chain solutions available anywhere in the world.
