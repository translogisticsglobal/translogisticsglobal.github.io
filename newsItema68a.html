TransGroup Opens New Station in Orlando, FL (MCO)

TransGroup Worldwide Logistics, a Seattle-based full service Domestic and International Freight Forwarder and Third-Party Logistics Provider (3PL), is pleased to announce the opening of their newest station, TransGroup-MCO, located in Orlando, Florida.

TransGroup-MCO operates as a full-service international and domestic freight forwarder and logistics provider. Station management is run by Dave Brewer, an industry veteran with over 37 years experience. Dave began his career in 1977 with BAX Global in Dayton, Ohio, then worked subsequently with Lynden and Associated Global. He is extensively experienced with FCL imports and in and handling the unique challenges of Trade Show logistics. Dave is excited about his latest challenge of growing TransGroup in the MCO market.

TransGroup-MCO is located at 5401 S Kirkman Rd, Suite 310, Orlando, FL 32819. The station can be reached by phone at TF: (888) 227-0032 / Lcl: (407) 926-5769, or via e-mail at mco@transgroup.com.
