Trucker Strike at Port of Ningbo, China

Activity at the Port of Ningbo has been severely impacted due to a labor dispute that began earlier this week. Hundreds of container truck drivers began to strike citing their transportation fees have remained level over the past 8 years despite the cost of fuel doubling in that same time period.

Demonstrators are attempting to stop passing container trucks near Beilun Port, south of Shanghai. The sudden escalation has even prompted over one hundred police, armed with riot gear, to try and contain the situation after several acts of violence were reported.

Cargo delays are expected and even if the strike were to end now the backup will take some time to resolve. TransGroup will continue to the monitor the situation and keep you posted of further developments. If you have any questions regarding your freight, please contact your local TransGroup station.
