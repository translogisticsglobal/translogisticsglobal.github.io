New Regulations for Inbound Shipments to China

Effective from June 1st, 2018 (onboard date), air and ocean shipments to China will be required to provide additional details on bills of lading including a more detailed cargo description, full contact information of the shipper and consignee parties, as well as the company registration numbers for all parties (EIN, VAT, Corporation Number, USCC).
